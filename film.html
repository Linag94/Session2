Film

Students learned the basics and foundations of film by using camera angles, camera shots, lighting, stop animation, storyboarding, directing, special effects and editing to create their own stories and visual art. The class worked collectively together and formulated their own idea to create a "Kids News" TV style show and worked with a fellow classmate to help them create an "Esngcape Room" film by playing different roles in front of the camera and on set.
